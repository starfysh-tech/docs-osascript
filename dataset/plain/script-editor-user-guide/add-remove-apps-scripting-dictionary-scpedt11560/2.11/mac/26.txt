Add or remove apps from the scripting dictionary in Script Editor on Mac

Open an app’s AppleScript dictionary by choosing the app from the Script Editor library.

Open Script Editor for me

In the Script Editor app
 on your Mac, choose Window > Library.

Do any of the following:

Add an app to the library:
 Click the Add button
 in the toolbar, choose the app, then click Open.

Delete an app from the library:
  Select the app, then click the Remove button
 in the toolbar.
